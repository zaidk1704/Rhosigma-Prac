What We DO

At Rhosigma, we offer a spectrum of services to elevate your electronic innovations. From Firmware and Hardware Development to end-to-end Product Development, we bring your ideas to market. Our expertise spans Reverse Engineering, Product Testing & Certification, and specialized areas like HART and AI/ML applications. With a focus on Manufacturing Support, we ensure efficient production processes.

Agile Methodology

In the ever-evolving landscape of technological innovation, Rhosigma embraces the Agile methodology. Here, we forge a collaborative alliance with our clients, facilitating iterative progress and adaptability to changing requirements. Through regular reviews, continuous communication, and incremental releases, our Agile approach ensures that our clients actively participate in shaping the evolving product, resulting in a finely tuned solution that mirrors their dynamic needs.

Waterfall Methodology

For projects demanding a structured and linear trajectory, Rhosigma excels in the Waterfall methodology. Our journey through design, development, and testing follows a systematic and sequential path, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of project requirements and objectives. This methodology is ideal for projects with well-defined goals and limited anticipated changes during the development phase, providing a structured framework for precision and clarity.
